    若A是n阶正定矩阵,则方程组AX=0的解只有0解。

      特征多项式

      $A \in \mathbb{R}$,多项式$p(\lambda)=det(\lambda I-A)$称为A的特征多项式（Characteristic polynomial），方程$p(\lambda)=0$称为特征方程（Characteristic equation），若$\lambda$是$p$的根，则$\lambda$是A的特征值，若v是满足$Av = \lambda v$的非零列向量，则v是A的一个特征值。

    矩阵逻辑链

    Variance

    covariance

    In probability theory and statistics, covariance is a measure of the joint variability of two random variables,which is suitable both for scalar and matrix and vector.
    $$
    cov(X,Y) = E[X-E[X])(Y-E[y])=E[XY]-E[X]E[Y]
    $$

    covariance matrix

    if the entries in the collumn vector are random variables,then we have:
    $$
    \bf{X} = [X_1,\cdots,X_n]^T \
    \Sigma_ij = cov(X_i,X_j)
    $$

    Variance

    It’s a scalar. In probability theory and statistics, variance is the expectation of the squared deviation of a random variable from its mean, and it informally measures how far a set of (random) numbers are spread out from their mean.
    $$
    Var(X) = E[(X-\mu)^2] = Cov(X,X)
    $$

    Matrix notation for the variance of a linear combination

    \blod{c} is a column vector of n scalars $c_1,\cdots,c_n$.Then we have:
    $$
    Var(c^TX) = c^T\Sigma c
    $$
    where $\Sigma$ is the covariance matrix of $X$.
